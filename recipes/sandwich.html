Sandwich Recipes

A quick and tasty sandwich made with fresh bread, veggies, and your choice of filling. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a light dinner. Ready in just minutes!

Ingredients

  • Bread Slices
  • Cheese
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Place cheese and tomato between two bread slices.
  2. Press the sandwich together.
  3. Serve as is or toast if desired
